Large Yama and niyama (in chapter 1)

1.16*1

अहिंसा सत्यमस्तेयं ब्रह्मचर्यं क्षमा धृतिः।

दयार्जवं मिताहारः शौचं चैव यमा दश॥

ahiṃsā satyam asteyaṃ brahmacaryaṃ kṣamā dhṛtiḥ /

dayārjavaṃ mitāhāraḥ śaucaṃ caiva yamā daśa //

The ten observances are non-violence, truthfulness, not stealing, celibacy, patience, resolve, compassion, sincerity, moderate eating, and cleanliness.

1.16*2

तपः संतोषमास्तिक्यं दानमीश्वरपूजनम्।

सिद्धान्तश्रवणं चैव ह्रीर्मतिश्च जपो हुतम्॥

tapaḥ saṃtoṣam āstikyaṃ dānam īśvarapūjanam /

siddhāntaśravaṇaṃ caiva hrīr matiś ca japo hutam //

[The ten rules are] asceticism, contentment, piety, charity, worship of God, listening to doctrinal teachings, compunction, contemplation, mantra recitation, and making offerings into a fire.
